<h1 align="center">Hi 👋, I'm Dimitris</h1>

A passionate **hardware design engineer** with a background in FPGA design, SoC integration, and processor architectures. I hold an Integrated Master’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTH) and am currently pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science at the University of Crete.

- 🔭 I’m currently working on the [REBECCA Project](https://www.rebecca-chip.eu/) (Reconfigurable Heterogeneous Highly Parallel Processing Platform for Safe and Secure AI).
- 🌱 My current research focuses on **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) accelerators**, which is also the subject of my master’s thesis.
